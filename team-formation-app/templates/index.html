📖 User Guide

  • Create Team: Click "Create Team", enter team name and secret key
  • Add Members: Enter last 3 digits of USN to auto-fill details
  • Team Size: Must have 3-4 members
  • Edit Team: Enter team number and secret key to modify
  • Secret Key: Keep it safe - you'll need it to edit your team
  • View Teams: Click "View Teams" to see all formed teams

💡 Quick Tips
